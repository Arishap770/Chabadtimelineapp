Privacy Policy for Chabad Timeline

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Chabad Timeline ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Feedback & Contact Information: When you submit feedback through our app, we collect your name and email address.
  • Notification Preferences: We store your notification settings locally on your device.
  • Language Preferences: Your selected language is stored locally on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the app (e.g., features accessed, time spent).
  • Location Data: When you use the Maps feature, we access your approximate location to show nearby event locations. This is only used when you actively use the Maps feature and is not stored or transmitted.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide App Functionality: To deliver core features including timeline browsing, event notifications, and map services.
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences (language, notification settings) and provide a personalized experience.
  • Communication: To respond to your feedback and support requests.
  • Improvements: To analyze app usage and improve features and performance.
  • Notifications: To send you notifications about upcoming Chabad events based on your preferences.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

3.1 EmailJS

  • Used for: Processing feedback submissions
  • Data shared: Name, email, and message content when you submit feedback
  • Privacy Policy: EmailJS Privacy Policy

3.2 Expo Services

  • Used for: App development, notifications, and analytics
  • Data shared: Device information, app usage data
  • Privacy Policy: Expo Privacy Policy

3.3 CashApp (Optional)

  • Used for: Processing donations (if you choose to donate)
  • We do not process or store any payment information
  • Privacy Policy: CashApp Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Most of your data (preferences, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect information transmitted through the app, but no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your preferences at any time through the Settings screen.
  • Location Data: You can disable location access through your device settings. This will prevent the Maps feature from showing your location.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications at any time through the app Settings or your device settings.
  • Delete Data: You can clear cached data through the Settings screen. Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. International Users

Our app is accessible worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
